About RazumAI

RazumAI (Razum GmbH, Switzerland) builds GxP-grade AI for pharmaceutical manufacturing, combining program leadership with hands-on ML engineering.

Our federated approach ensures that while raw data never leaves a company or site, every interaction contributes to improving the shared base model. In this way, each client benefits not only from their own training but also from accumulated “lessons learned” across the network — much like how Google Search improves continuously from user interactions. RazumAI data-quality solutions improve continuously as they are deployed across pharma, becoming more accurate and reliable for all participants.

What we do

  • Run the GenAI Recipe Audit Benchmark for ALCOA+ compliance checks.
  • Deliver Validated RAG with full interaction history and evidence links.
  • Guide teams to validated deployment (CSV/CSA, risk-based).

Why it matters

  • Explainable, documented, and inspectable AI for pharma.
  • Benchmarks make model choices defendable.
  • Traceability reduces risk and accelerates adoption.
