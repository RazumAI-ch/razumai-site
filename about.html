About RazumAI

“Pilots are easy, production is hard — what matters most is the speed of innovation.”

Continuous interactions with our system make the system smarter over time. Each correction, each flagged record, and each validation helps the AI recognize similar issues more effectively in the future.

This creates a network effect: the data itself never leaves your environment, but the learnings do. Every site and every company benefits from what others have taught the system. In-house tools can fix local problems, but they cannot share knowledge industry-wide.

This is possible because our solutions are federated by design. Data stays on-prem or in your secure cloud, while only learned patterns about data quality are shared — never proprietary content or IP. Much like Google Search improves with every user interaction, our base model becomes stronger as more companies use it — continuously improving pharma’s ability to safeguard data quality and patient safety.

Curious fact — our logo

Our logo is inspired by Pandora’s box and the force that had been hidden inside it is AI. The lid is now open: for good or for bad, AI is out in the world and there is no going back. At RazumAI, we are here to help pharma harness that energy — in a safe and responsible way.

The inside of the box is rendered in orange to symbolize AI’s transformative energy, while the outside of the box is done in blue to reflect pharma’s rigor and care. Subtle artistic impressions of the letters R and AI are embedded in the outer walls, referencing the company’s name.

The lid features the structure of myoglobin — the first protein structure ever solved. Its inclusion pays homage to Google DeepMind’s AlphaFold, which showed that AI can solve real-life critical challenges in pharma, such as protein folding, profoundly shaping modern drug discovery.
