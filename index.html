Our mission: Accelerating AI adoption in pharma

AI adoption in pharma

Pharma manufacturers are adopting AI, but mostly via generic internet-trained models. For GxP quality, that’s like asking a well-read outsider for regulatory advice — fine for basics, not for essential decisions. Mission-critical tasks need purpose-trained pharma AI.

The Gap

Big tech can train generative AI models, but they lack deep pharma expertise. Pharma companies have that expertise, but not AI scale or the specialized talent required to train models.

The RazumAI Approach

RazumAI bridges the gap by training generative AI models on real pharma manufacturing data, partnering with leading pharma companies for domain expertise and Google for technological excellence.

Since data quality is a regulatory baseline, pooling resources makes sense. Compute costs are straightforward to share — but the real advantage is talent. Top AI specialists seek impact beyond a single company, and RazumAI gives them that scope, delivering faster, higher‑quality, and more cost‑effective solutions for the entire industry.

Backed by the Google for Startups Cloud Program, we provide the infrastructure and expertise to develop and benchmark industry‑grade models. Every validation, correction, and flagged issue strengthens the system, so all participants benefit from the collective effort to improve data quality — while their own data never leaves their premises. It’s collective intelligence without IP exposure.

Vision

Within 3–5 years, AI‑powered data quality validation will be 10× better and 10× cheaper than manual methods. We're building this future with forward‑thinking pharma partners.
