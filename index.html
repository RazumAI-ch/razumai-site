Our mission: Accelerating AI adoption in pharma

With every click, you make patients safer — and improve data quality for everyone. Your data stays in your secure environment, while the learnings are shared so quality improves across the industry. Every interaction teaches the system to recognise and explain issues more effectively over time.

Our privacy-preserving, pharma-grade AI for GxP data validation is trained on production customer data, adapts to site-specific needs, and generalizes across new companies. That means you don’t start from a blank slate: even a plain-vanilla pilot can deliver something meaningful on day one. If you need more, we specialize the model to your data — and the more your teams use the system, the better it gets at spotting and explaining issues across your full stack.

AI will eventually make data quality checks an order of magnitude better and an order of magnitude cheaper. Humans can only review a limited set of records at a time, and there are only so many highly trained experts — people who take years to develop. AI will eventually be able to check all the data, all the time, in real time: it never tires, can hold vastly larger volumes of data in memory, and operates at unmatched speeds — catching issues humans cannot. It becomes 10× better (more reliable and more effective) and 10× cheaper because it frees scarce experts to focus on higher-value, more sophisticated tasks instead of routine checks.
