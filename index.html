Our mission: Accelerating AI adoption in pharma

Pharma manufacturers are already eagerly adopting AI in their operations. We are here to take that adoption to the next level — accelerating it further, and making it more efficient and more impactful. We multiply AI gains by raising the level of sophistication that is generally not easily attainable by individual pharma industry players.

Pharma implementations today rely on API access to generic LLMs, combined with sophisticated prompting — the equivalent of asking a well-read person from the street for advice on pharma production. You might listen, but you wouldn’t rely on that advice to make critical decisions about data quality. For that, you want experts — and in pharma AI, that means generative AI models built on insider pharma knowledge. Big tech can train such generative AI models but lack insider pharma expertise. Individual pharma companies have manufacturing expertise but not AI scale or the ability to attract the specialized talent needed to train generative AI models.

We aim to fill that gap: RazumAI spreads the cost of compute needed for generative AI models training and attracts top AI talent by focusing exclusively on AI — something individual pharma companies are generally not well positioned to do. Our solution is built on generative AI models custom-trained on proprietary, real production data from pharma manufacturing.

We are at the beginning of this journey, but in 3-5 years AI data quality checks will be 10× better and 10× cheaper than today’s legacy methods of checking data quality.
